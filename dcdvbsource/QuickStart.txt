1) Execute "register.bat" in the "Filter" Directory
2) Execute "register.bat" in the "Plugins" Directory
3) Execute "ChannelScan.exe" in the "Filter" Directory
4) Go to the "Channel Scan" Tab 
5) Choose the Device and Frequency List you want to Scan
6) Execute the Scan and wait for it to end
7) Go to the "Decoder" Tab and choose an Audio and Video Decoder
8) Save everything in to a .dvb File
9) Open the previously saved .dvb File in your Media Player and Enjoy
